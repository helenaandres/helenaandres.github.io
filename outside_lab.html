Outside the lab

When not working you will most likely find me chasing flying discs around the planet. I am an Ultimate Frisbee athlete, representing Spain with the Spanish Ultimate Frisbee Women National Team and also playing for the London based club team, SYC . Although I do most of my training on grass, my heart mostly belongs to beach Ultimate, where I have also played both european and world championships.

I am engaged with a few communities and organisations to promote women in STEM and Sports. I do my best to promote equity and equal opportunities for women & NB folk, raising awarness of gender issues both on and off the field, as well as in education and the workplace.

If I am not running around muddy fields or sandy beaches I enjoy cycling, climbing and hiking - sometimes even all together. I am always keen to explore and start new adventures, the harder the challenge the better.
